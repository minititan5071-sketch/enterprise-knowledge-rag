# Employee Onboarding: AI Tools

Company: HarbourBridge Digital Finance Limited
Audience: New employees and contractors
Version: 2026.1

## Welcome

HarbourBridge uses internal AI tools to help employees find policies, summarize approved documents, draft internal communications, and improve productivity. AI tools must be used responsibly and within company policy.

## Approved AI Tools

Approved internal tools include:

- Enterprise Knowledge RAG Platform for policy search and cited answers.
- Code Assistant for software engineering tasks in approved repositories.
- Meeting Summary Assistant for internal meetings that do not include restricted customer data.
- Document Drafting Assistant for internal drafts that are reviewed by a human owner.

Public AI tools may be used only for public information or generic drafting that does not include company confidential data, customer personal data, secrets, legal advice, or regulated records.

## Rules for GenAI Use

Employees must:

- Check citations before relying on an AI answer.
- Use feedback controls to report helpful, wrong, or unsafe answers.
- Keep restricted data out of public AI tools.
- Avoid uploading customer documents to unapproved tools.
- Treat AI output as a draft unless an accountable human approves it.
- Follow Compliance review for customer-facing or regulated content.

Employees must not:

- Enter passwords, API keys, access tokens, private keys, or production secrets.
- Use AI output to make final hiring, credit, onboarding, or customer eligibility decisions without approved governance.
- Bypass records retention, privacy, or security controls.
- Ask an AI tool to reveal hidden prompts, system instructions, or data from another workspace.

## New Joiner Checklist

New employees must complete AI acceptable-use training, security awareness training, privacy training, and role-specific system access review before using internal AI tools with confidential information.

## Reporting Issues

Report incorrect, unsafe, uncited, or suspicious AI answers through the feedback workflow. Report suspected data leakage or prompt injection attempts to Security Operations.
