# Digital Banking Product FAQ

Company: HarbourBridge Digital Finance Limited
Product: HarbourBridge Business Wallet
Audience: Customer-facing and internal teams
Version: 2026.1

## What is HarbourBridge Business Wallet?

HarbourBridge Business Wallet is a fictional digital banking product for small and medium-sized companies. It supports multi-currency balances, local transfers, payment approvals, transaction exports, and user permission management.

## Who can apply?

Hong Kong registered companies, sole proprietors, and approved offshore entities may apply. All applicants must complete KYC checks, sanctions screening, and product eligibility review before account activation.

## What currencies are supported?

The demo product supports HKD, USD, CNY, EUR, and GBP balances. Currency availability may vary by customer risk profile, product configuration, and banking partner availability.

## How do payment approvals work?

Workspace admins can configure approval rules by amount, currency, and payment type. A common setup requires one approver for transfers below HKD 50,000 and two approvers for transfers at or above HKD 50,000.

## What happens when a payment fails?

A payment may fail because of invalid recipient details, insufficient funds, bank rejection, compliance review, duplicate detection, or a payment network incident. Support agents must follow the failed transfer handling process in the Customer Support SOP.

## Are customers charged for returned transfers?

Returned transfer fees depend on the payment rail and banking partner. Agents should refer to the current fee schedule and avoid promising fee waivers unless approved by Customer Operations.

## Can customers export transaction data?

Customers can export transaction history in CSV format from the dashboard. Large exports may require additional verification or operations support.

## Product Risk Notes

The product is not a stored value facility in this fictional scenario and does not offer investment returns. Customer communications must not imply guaranteed returns, deposit protection, or regulatory endorsement.
